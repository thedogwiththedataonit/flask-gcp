Why

Datadog?

Agent Installation

While GCP can monitor both hybrid and multi-cloud environments through their agents, Datadog has more integrations.


No Synthetics or RUM

GCP does not offer any synthetics or Real User Monitoring. Limited support any front-end monitoring solutions like Datadog.


Superior DevOps Communciation

Datadog offer public invite urls which allows anyone on any team to gain value from the platform. Alerts, incidents, and graphs can be shared within the platform and even across Pagerduty, Slack, ServiceNow.


Unified Tagging Structure

GCP requires a query builder (menu selector and writing queries) to filter through logs. Datadog uses a unified tagging and facet structure that seamlessly filters through logs and ties them to relevant instances or services.


Superior UI

GCP Monitor's dashboards and UI are nothing special.
